Solution Overview

Notes or summaries are regular things that students require to remember and understand things learnt earlier. Just when you need them though, you can't seem to find them and then rewrite them or make do without it. If such learning nuggets were sorted, portable and updated regularly, life would be much easier and more so if your teachers update it. ReCapp is a such a platform which aims to give you this carefully extracted knowledge, anytime and anywhere. With ReCapp, an institution can give the teachers more control over the subject and maximize learning and an individual gets a platform to consolidate their notes and access them whenever they want.

Who All Can Use It
Institutional User

Available to all types of teachers, an institutional subscription would allow the teachers to create and publish their lessons under a single platform. Students assigned to specific teachers get automatic updates and the content can be downloaded or streamed according to their preference.

Individual Tutors can use this platform to publish their content and expand their market base.

Individual User

As a student attached to an institution which avail to the platform you receive content directly from the Teachers. You may add to this but will not be able to change it.

You get the option of creating your own content in the 'Own' tab. This is locally stored and can be changed or deleted according to your will.

As a Direct Individual User you can create content in the “Own” tab.

What Can You Create

Learning is primarily done through observing and listening. Therefore, it is important to know what to focus on and what to take away from a topic.

Introducing the Learning Droplet – This unit of learning is done by a set of Images or a hyperlink of a video, audio, animation or graphic or a set of Step-by-Step Solution images.

There are 4 types of Droplets that we provide – Definitions, Explanations, Summaries or Q&As.

There are 2 models of Learning Solutions possible – The Direct Approach or the Step-by-Step approach.

There is no limit on the range of subjects that can be covered, on the language of instruction, to the type of skills that you want to impart and to the amount of content you want to create.

How To Create – The Basics
As a Teacher or Tutor or Trainer

After initial planning, it’s like taking a short class. Every Learning Droplet has a Header/Question after which the detailed answer follows.

As preparation you can download or click a picture of the written solution. You can include a video or start recording audio. Choose between using a direct or a step-by-step approach.

Every droplet is a recap. Upload and the students assigned to you can access it. If any changes are required, delete the old version and upload new. Click, Speak and Publish.

As a Student

If you belong to an institution that uses ReCapp, you can avail 2 options.

In the ‘Institution’ tab, use what the teacher has published and add your own Droplet, within the structure of the course. Under the ‘Own’ tab, you can create content, which is stored locally and cannot be shared.

As a direct individual user, you can create under the 'Own' tab.

After basic planning, use the 'Own' tab. After giving a header, attach a video or record audio and answer directly or in a step-by-step manner. Effective learning is now easy.

How To Create – The Process Flow
As a Teacher or Tutor or Trainer

After logging in, select your class and subject, and create a Title and topic. Select the type of Droplet and go ahead with the recording. You can publish it immediately, do all recordings and publish or you can choose to publish at an appropriate time. Students assigned to you can only access the droplet once you hit publish.

How To Use It for Learning

Learning is now available at your fingertips. Login and start learning – Anytime and Anywhere.

You have 3 options for Learning:

Single Unit Option - Go to the specific Learning Droplet and hit play; the recording will replay.

Revise Option - Select an entire bunch of droplets and the entire content will replay one after the other. You can choose autoplay or move manually. Revising has never been easier.

Recap Option - Create an exam simulation by selecting multiple droplets. The content gets randomized, after which header plays first and you can answer and then compare it to the original body.
